LTC-637AX-12 LED Clock Display

Preamble

This LED clock display was pulled from a Spartan alarm clock (figure 1). The display is coupled to a TI clock-radio chip, the TMS3450NL. The display is marked as LTC-637AX-12, with the text T8430 printed beneath the part number.

Figure 2 shows the segment labels used to identify each digit's segments. Figure 3 shows the overall dispaly layout with labels to identify sections. Figure 4 shows the roughly 0.1" spacing pin header on the module. Note that pins 11 and 14 are not soldered onto the module's PCB. Table 1 shows the pin mapping for this display.
